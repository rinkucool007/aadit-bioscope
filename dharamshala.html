Trip To Dharamshala

Aadit Mohapatra Blogger
16th June, 2023
#Travel #Flight #Vlogger

Hi everyone I am Aadit and I am going to share my Dharamshala trip experience.

My trip so far was until 4 days so we had lot of fun

Day1:

We (Family) took a flight from Delhi to Dharamshala (DEL-DHA). Then we took a hotel in a beautiful place called mecloydgunz. We saw the beauty of the mountains there and clicked pics through the window. We rested in our hotel room for a few mins and then went for dinner in the evening. We filled our stomach in good and delicious food. That made our sleep very good.

Day2:

We woke up and there was a pleasant weather better than before. We had breakfast before our tour in Dharamshala places. We went to a small tea garden and it was very pleasant look. We went to a very big place and had our lunch there in a place called Norbolinka institute and clicked photos there. We reached the hotel and had some snacks. We went for dinner in sometime and after we went from dinner. We went to a small room called a fire place we took photos in it. We went to the room and rested in peace.

Day3:

We woke up once again in a pleasant weather. We went to a monastery and took some photos and videos and saw that there are some men who are worshiping the man Dalai Lama who is very important in Dharamshala. We went to hotel back and ate some snacks and rested for some time.

Day4:

We did not go anywhere we packed for the flight tomorrow and went to lunch and dinner along and watched a movie in the hotels home cinema then came back in the room in the evening in bedtime we watched another movie and slept throughout the night.

Day5:

It was our time for the flight from Dharamshala to Delhi (DHA-DEL) we miss Dharamshala and we will never forget the peace and quietness that we have brought along :)

Keep encourging me reading my experience and I will share more in my next blog
